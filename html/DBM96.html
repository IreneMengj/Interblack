Sintered Magnesia DBM 96 - Technical Requirements

Sintered Magnesia DBM 96 contains magnesium oxide (MgO) of no less than 96%. High-purity magnesia is produced by selecting premium-grade natural magnesite ore. This ore undergoes flotation purification, light burning, fine grinding, briquetting, and ultra-high temperature calcination in an oil shaft kiln. The final product is high-quality magnesia with MgO content above 96%. It serves as an essential raw material for producing bricks and unshaped refractory materials.

Quality Characteristics

Indicator Name Unit DBM 96
MgO Content % ≥96.0
Sintering Degree (RUL) ℃ ≥1,680
Fe₂O₃ Content % ≤0.9
SiO₂ Content % ≤2.0
CaO Content % ≤1.5
Al₂O₃ Content % ≤0.4
Bulk Density g/cm³ ≥3.30
Apparent Porosity % ≤18.0

Application

Sintered Magnesia DBM 96 is primarily used in the production of high-grade refractory bricks and unshaped refractory materials. It is suitable for steelmaking processes and serves as an essential material for the lining of furnaces.

Packaging and Storage

The product is available in various packaging options, including 1-ton jumbo bags. It should be stored in a dry, ventilated place, and protected from moisture.
