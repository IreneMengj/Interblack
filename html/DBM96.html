Sintered Magnesia DBM 96

Sintered Magnesia DBM 96 is a high-purity magnesia product with magnesium oxide content no less than 96%. It is manufactured by selecting premium-grade natural magnesite ore, which undergoes flotation purification, light burning, fine grinding, briquetting, and ultra-high temperature calcination in an oil shaft kiln. This process results in a product with high purity, making it an excellent raw material for producing high-quality refractory materials.

Key Quality Characteristics:

  • MgO Content: ≥ 96%
  • SiO₂ Content: ≤ 0.8%
  • CaO Content: ≤ 1.5%
  • Fe₂O₃ Content: ≤ 0.8%
  • Al₂O₃ Content: ≤ 0.4%
  • Apparent Density: ≥ 3.24 g/cm³
  • Moisture Content: ≤ 1.0%
  • Open Porosity: ≤ 3.7%
  • Loss on Ignition: ≤ 0.45%
  • Particle Size Distribution: 0-1 mm ≤ 10%, 1-30 mm ≥ 90%

Applications:

This product is ideal for producing high-grade magnesia refractory materials, including:

  • Magnesia bricks
  • Magnesia-chrome bricks
  • Magnesia-carbon bricks
  • Tundish ramming mass
  • Magnesia castables
