FM97.3LC (Large Crystal)

Fused Magnesia FM 97.3 LC is a high-quality magnesia-based refractory material with a magnesium oxide (MgO) content of no less than 97.3%. It is used in the production of various refractory products, particularly where high crystal stability and specific chemical characteristics are required.

Technical Requirements

FM97.3LC (Large Crystal)

Indicator Requirements
MgO ≥97.3%
SiO2 ≤0.8%
CaO ≤1.4%
Fe2O3 ≤0.7%
Al2O3 ≤0.2%
Apparent Density ≥3.48 g/cm³
Open Porosity ≤2.3%
Loss on Ignition ≤0.2%

Particle Size Requirements

Size Range Percentage
1-10 mm ≤10%
10-40 mm ≥90%

Crystal Size Requirements

Crystal Size Percentage
800-1000 μm ≤5%
Larger than 1000 μm ≥95%

Appearance

The material should consist of monolithic structures with high crystalline stability and a characteristic luster ranging in color from white to gray, without yellow or animal-like streaks. No cracks, foreign matter, or other defects are allowed. The material should not have any significant odor.

Packaging Requirements

The product should be packed in clean big bags with dimensions of 50x200 cm and a weight of 1.5 tons per bag. These bags ensure protection during transportation and storage. Transportation can be carried out by rail or road, ensuring the integrity of the packaging.
