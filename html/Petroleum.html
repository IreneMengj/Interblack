HFO 180 Fuel Oil Technical Data

Fuel Oil is a fraction obtained from petroleum distillation either as a distillate or a residue. It is broadly used in power generation, marine bunkers, and large industrial heating plants.

Applications:

  • Power generation
  • Marine bunkers
  • Large industrial heating plants
