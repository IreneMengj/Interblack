Silica Brick Technical Data

This section provides technical specifications, tolerances, and physical/chemical properties for silica refractory bricks.

1. Tolerances

Dimension Tolerance
Products up to 100mm (inclusive) +1; -2mm
Products from 101 to 350mm (inclusive) ± 2mm
More than 350mm ± 3mm
Flow control devices and outlet section products ± 1mm

2. Permissible Damage to Edges and Corners

Surface/Part Damage Tolerance
Sides exposed to coal or gases A ≤ 2mm, B ≤ 2mm, C ≤ 2mm, D ≥ 20mm
Sides in contact with the coal charge A ≤ 2mm, B ≤ 2mm, C ≤ 2mm
Other sides A ≤ 3mm, B ≤ 3mm, C ≤ 3mm, D ≥ 20mm

Cracks and Spalling:

  • Surface cracks with a length of 0.1-0.3 mm on the fire side and the side to be covered by brickwork are not permitted.
  • Spalling on surfaces in contact with coke and gas is not permitted.

3. Physical and Chemical Properties

Parameter Limit
Al2O3 (Mass Fraction %) Max 1.5
SiO2 (Mass Fraction %) Max 94
Fe2O3 (Mass Fraction %) Max 1.7
CaO (Mass Fraction %) Max 3.0
K2O+Na2O (Mass Fraction %) Max 0.35
Density (g/cm³) Max 2.37
Compressive strength (N/mm²) Max 3.5
Porosity (%) Max 22
Softening point under a load of 0.2N/mm² (°C) Max 1650°C
Additional expansion at 1450°C (%) Max 0.3%
Residual quartz content (%) Max 6.0%

4. List of Silica Refractories

The following are the required specifications for different grades of silica refractories:

  • Sole Products:
    • Open porosity: max 16%
    • Compressive strength: min 50 MPa
    • Grades: 18402, 18401, 20219
  • Other Silica Refractories:
    • Open porosity: max 22%
    • Compressive strength: min 35 MPa
