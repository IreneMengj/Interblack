Pork Feet (Grade A)

Pork feet, also known as trotters, are widely recognized for their high collagen content, making them a preferred ingredient in various international cuisines. They are typically slow-cooked or braised to produce a tender and flavorful dish, rich in nutrients.

Key Features:

  • Grade A quality: No hair, no bruising, no skin damage, bright color
  • Rich in collagen and essential nutrients
  • Perfect for slow-cooking, braising, and used in traditional dishes worldwide
  • Frozen and stored under optimal conditions

Weight: 1kg - 5kg per pack

Price: $15 per kg

Import Documentation:

For customs clearance of imported frozen pork feet, the following documentation is required:

  • Origin certificate, health certificate, factory registration number, Chinese labels, packing list, invoice, bill of lading, and contract from the exporter
  • Import license, quarantine inspection permit for entry animals/plants, and importer registration number from the importer

Customs Clearance Process:

The customs clearance for frozen pork feet involves:

  • Preparing import documents in advance: factory number, origin certificate
  • Providing product details such as name, brand, production date, preservation method, and grade for the creation of Chinese labels for inspection
  • Delivering frozen pork feet to the designated port for concentrated supervision and reporting to inspection authorities
  • Conducting on-site inspections and document reviews by customs authorities
  • Random sampling for safety checks; issuance of an "Entry Goods Quarantine Certificate" upon successful inspection
  • Declaring the goods to customs, paying the applicable tariffs, and arranging delivery after customs clearance

Import Duties: Frozen pork feet, duty rate 12%, VAT 9%
