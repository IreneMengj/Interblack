Pork Feet

Pork feet, also known as trotters, are a popular ingredient in many cuisines around the world. They are prized for their rich collagen content, which makes them ideal for slow-cooking methods such as braising. The resulting dish is tender, flavorful, and full of nutrients.

Key Features:

  • Rich in collagen and nutrients
  • Perfect for slow-cooking and braising
  • Used in various traditional dishes globally
